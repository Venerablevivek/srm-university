SRM KTR B. Tech Direct Admission

  • SRM University Chennai stands for Sri Ramaswami Memorial University and is a deemed university privately owned in the state of Tamilnadu.
  • In the year 1985, around 7 campuses had been started as an engineering college in Kattankulathur, under the University of Madras.
  • In 2001, this campus became a separate university and was named after its founder Sri Ramaswami Mudaliar.
  • SRM has been listed among the top ten universities in India and also ranked among the top 500 universities of the world by QS World Rankings 2020.
  • The university offers courses at graduate, postgraduate, doctoral, and research levels. It has over 200 affiliated institutions spread across India and overseas campuses in Dubai, Malaysia, and Bhutan.
  • SRM University was established to promote excellence in higher education, research, and development in all fields of knowledge through quality teaching and learning processes.
  • They provide opportunities for leadership development through interdisciplinary studies and foster national integration through developing regional centers across India.
  • They promote entrepreneurship by providing business incubation facilities, encourage academic excellence by promoting research activities, and encourage cultural diversity through globalization initiatives.
  • They always try to give a boost to internationalization with students from different parts of the world studying here; create opportunities for self-employment for rural youth through skill development programs and give equal opportunities to women.

Admission Process for B. Tech Direct Admission in SRM Chennai

  • Go to the official website of SRM Chennai. The candidate has to register by filling in the required details of the form.
  • The verification mail will be sent to your registered email id. You have to verify your details from there.
  • After login, fill up the application form and select the course of your choice.
  • Upload the required documents mentioned in the list, with the application form.
  • An application number will be generated after the application form has been successfully submitted.
  • Pay the required fee of application on the given bank details. The candidate can also pay the application fee in the form of DD addressing SRMIST and payable in Chennai.

Eligibility for B. Tech Direct Admission in SRM Chennai

  • Indian residents or Non-Resident Indians (NRI), who are holding PIO or OCI cards issued by the Government of India can apply for Selection Process.
  • The age of the candidate should be more than 16 years at the time of application.
  • The candidate should have passed the 10th and 12th exams with 60% of the marks.
  • The candidate must appear in SRMJEEE/SRMAPET exams. The selection is based on SRMJEEE/SRMAPET scores.
  • To apply for B.tech in  Genetic Engineering, Biotechnology Engineering and Biomedical Engineering program 50% total mark either in PCM or PCB (Physics, Chemistry, Biology).

Direct Admission in SRM Chennai Through Management Quota

  • SRM B.Tech/ B.Arch/ B.Design Admission after SRMJEEE exams through leftover seats from counseling in a low budget package.
  • Canceled and returned seats are left which anyone can join if applied for the entrance exams with the help of consultancy.
  • Regular Admission: If you have passed 12th with minimum 50% marks in Mathematics, Physics and Chemistry and scored at least 60% marks in aggregate in PCM/ PCB or Biology /Biotechnology/microbiology or Botany, Zoology /Molecular Biology & Biotechnology then you can apply for regular admission.
  • Management Quota Admission: If you have cleared JEE Main Advanced exam with either AIR 1-200 then you can directly join SRM University under management quota seats by filling up the application form available on official website of SRM University, Chennai.

Placements for B.Tech in SRM Chennai

SRM Institute of Science and Technology (SRM IST) Chennai is one of the top colleges in India. It has been ranked as one of the best engineering colleges in Tamil Nadu. The placement department of SRMIST Chennai plays a vital role in helping students get placed into reputed companies. It is known as the SRM Career Centre.

The SRMIST Chennai placement department is also responsible for training the students so that they are placed into reputed companies. Various training programmes are organized by the SRM IST placement department to help students meet industry requirements.

The SRMIST Chennai placement office works out various ways to help students get placed in top companies every year. The campus recruitment process involves an interview round where candidates are shortlisted based on their performance during the interview and group discussion sessions. Candidates are also given an aptitude test so that they can be assessed according to their skills and knowledge levels.

The SRMIST Chennai placement office organizes various events like workshops, seminars and guest lectures which help students enhance their knowledge levels and understand industry requirements better than before. These events also help students understand what sort of skills are required by employers in todays competitive world.
